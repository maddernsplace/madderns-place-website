About Us

Professional Landscaping Across Adelaide's South

At Maddern's Place Garden & Landscaping, we deliver professional landscaping services across Hackham, Morphett Vale, and the wider Adelaide southern suburbs. Whether you're upgrading your front yard, building a retaining wall, or laying premium turf, we create stunning, functional outdoor spaces tailored to your needs.

As a trusted landscaping business in Adelaide, we specialise in:

  • Landscape design and construction
  • Retaining wall installation
  • New turf installation (Kikuyu, Sir Walter, and more)
  • Irrigation systems using Hunter MP Rotators and Bluetooth NODE timers
  • Lawn renovations and maintenance
  • Gutter vacuum cleaning services

With a strong focus on residential and commercial landscaping, we pride ourselves on attention to detail, reliable communication, and high-quality workmanship. Every project is managed with care to ensure your outdoor area not only looks great but adds real value to your property.

If you're searching for landscapers near me, turf installation in Adelaide, or irrigation experts in Hackham, you're in the right place.
