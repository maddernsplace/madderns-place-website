Your Local Hackham Landscaper

Maddern's Place Garden & Landscaping is based right in Hackham, which means we know the local area inside out. We understand the soil conditions, typical block layouts, and the challenges that come with landscaping in this part of Adelaide's south.

Hackham has a mix of established older homes and newer developments. Older properties often have tired gardens that benefit from a complete renovation — new turf, updated irrigation, retaining walls, and replanting. Newer estates come with bare blocks ready for a full build from the ground up.

Because we're local, you'll find us fast to respond, easy to reach, and genuinely invested in the quality of our work right here in our own community.

Every project starts with a no-obligation site visit and a clear, itemised quote — so you always know what you're getting before we start.
