What We Offer

Irrigation That Works Every Time

A well-designed irrigation system saves water, saves time, and keeps your lawn and garden looking their best through Adelaide's harsh summers. At Maddern's Place, we design, install, and repair irrigation systems for residential properties across the southern suburbs.

Whether you need a brand new system, an upgrade to your existing setup, or a fault diagnosed and fixed, our team delivers reliable solutions designed for efficient water use and healthy growth.

We work with all major irrigation brands and system types — from simple lawn sprinklers to fully zoned drip irrigation for complex garden layouts.

System Types

Irrigation Solutions for Every Application

Different areas of your property need different watering approaches. We design systems that match the right method to the right zone.

Pop-Up Lawn Sprinklers

Rotating and fixed pop-up heads for even lawn coverage — designed to retract flush with the turf surface when not in use.

Drip Lines for Garden Beds

Low-pressure drip lines that deliver water directly to plant root zones, reducing evaporation and keeping foliage dry to minimise disease.

Micro-Spray Systems

Mini sprinklers for garden beds with dense planting — providing even coverage over a wider area than drip emitters.

Multi-Zone Controllers

Programmable controllers managing multiple irrigation zones independently — letting lawn, beds, and pots each run on their own schedule.

Rainwater Tank Integration

Connecting your irrigation system to a rainwater tank to reduce mains water usage and keep your garden watered sustainably.

Pressure Management

Installing pressure regulators to protect your system from high mains pressure — extending the life of your heads, valves, and pipes.
