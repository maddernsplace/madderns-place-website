What We Offer

Your Outdoor Space, Completely Transformed

At Maddern's Place, we specialise in complete outdoor transformations — from a single feature like a retaining wall through to full garden builds including turf, irrigation, drainage, and planting.

We manage projects of all sizes across Hackham, Morphett Vale, and the wider Adelaide southern suburbs, handling every stage in-house so your project runs smoothly from start to finish.

Every job begins with a detailed site assessment and transparent quote, so you know exactly what to expect before work begins.

What We Do

Our Landscaping Services

🧱

Retaining Walls

Block and timber retaining walls designed for both function and aesthetics — solving level changes, improving drainage, and creating usable garden terraces.

🌿

Turf Installation

Supply and installation of fresh turf across all popular varieties. We prepare the ground properly so your new lawn establishes quickly and stays healthy.

💧

Irrigation Systems

Design and installation of efficient irrigation systems tailored to your garden layout — ensuring every area is covered without overwatering or waste.

🔩

Drainage Solutions

Surface and subsurface drainage to protect your property from waterlogging, soil erosion, and moisture damage — designed to last.

🌱

Planting & Mulching

Tailored planting of trees, shrubs, groundcovers, and feature plants — finished with quality mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds.

🛤️

Paving & Pathways

Installation of paving, gravel paths, and stepping stones that tie your outdoor space together and make it practical to navigate year-round.

Project Types

We Handle Projects of All Sizes

From small backyard refreshes to large-scale property transformations, we bring the same level of care and quality to every project.

New Build Landscaping

Turning a blank canvas into a finished outdoor space — we work with new homeowners to create gardens that complement the home from day one.

Garden Renovations

Breathing new life into tired or overgrown gardens — removing the old, replanning the layout, and installing a fresh, low-maintenance design.

Professional landscaping for commercial and rental properties — reliable, well-presented, and completed with minimal disruption to your tenants.
