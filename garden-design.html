What We Offer

Gardens Designed Around You

A beautiful garden starts with a great design. At Maddern's Place, we take the time to understand how you use your outdoor space — whether you need a low-maintenance retreat, a space for entertaining, or a garden full of colour and life.

We work with you from the initial consultation through to planting and completion, ensuring every detail reflects your vision and works with your site's conditions.

Servicing Hackham, Morphett Vale, and the wider Adelaide southern suburbs, our designers bring both creative flair and practical expertise to every project.

Our Garden Design Services

📋

On-Site Consultation

We meet at your property to assess the space, understand your vision, discuss your budget, and identify opportunities and challenges unique to your site.

✏️

Concept Design

A clear visual concept showing layout, planting zones, paving, and key features — giving you a tangible picture of your future garden before any work begins.

🌿

Planting Plans

Detailed planting plans that factor in your soil, aspect, water availability, and personal preferences — selecting species that thrive in Adelaide's climate.

🏗️

Hardscape Integration

Design of pathways, paving, retaining walls, and structures that complement your plantings and create a cohesive, functional outdoor space.

💧

Irrigation Planning

Incorporating water-efficient irrigation into the design from the start to ensure your plants thrive without wasted water or ongoing effort.

🔧

Full Installation

We don't just design — we build. Our team can manage the complete installation of your garden design, from soil preparation through to final planting and mulching.

Garden Styles

We Design Every Style

From lush and informal to clean and contemporary, we work across a wide range of garden styles to suit any home and lifestyle.

Cottage Gardens

Informal, romantic, and full of colour — cottage gardens layer perennials, roses, and herbs for a relaxed, abundant look that's always in season.

Contemporary Gardens

Clean lines, bold plantings, and thoughtful materials create striking modern gardens that complement contemporary architecture beautifully.

Low-Maintenance Gardens

Designed for real life — we select hardy, water-wise plants and smart layouts that look great without demanding constant attention.

Entertaining Spaces

Gardens designed around outdoor living — integrating alfresco areas, shade structures, and plantings that create a seamless indoor-outdoor connection.

Native Gardens

Celebrating Australian flora — native gardens attract wildlife, use minimal water, and connect your space to the local landscape with authenticity.

Kitchen & Herb Gardens

Functional and beautiful — we design edible gardens that integrate herbs, vegetables, and fruit seamlessly into your outdoor space.
